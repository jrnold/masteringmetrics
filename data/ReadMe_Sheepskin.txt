** ReadMe_Sheepskin.txt **

These files contain the data and programs used to generate Figures 6.3 and 6.4 in Mastering 'Metrics. These figures use a fuzzy RD design to analyze the "sheepskin effects" of a high school diploma.

Data: 
* /Sheepskin/Data/clark_martorell_cellmeans.dta

Code: 
* /Sheepskin/Code/cm_graphs.do

Output: 
* /Sheepskin/Output/fig1.png (Figure 6.3)
* /Sheepskin/Output/fig2.png (Figure 6.4)
