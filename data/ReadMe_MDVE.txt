** ReadMe_MDVE.txt **

These files contain the data and programs used to generate Table 3.3 in Mastering 'Metrics. This table tabulates police assignments and outcomes from the Minneapolis Domestic Violence Experiment. The tabulations are used to generate IV estimates of the "coddling" effect. 

Data: 
* /MDVE/Data/mdve.dta

Code: 
* /MDVE/Code/MDVE_Table33.do

Output: 
* /MDVE/Output/Table33.xls (Table 3.3)
